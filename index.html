What we do

One contractor, shell to finish

From stripping out to the final coat, JL Interiors covers every stage of a commercial fit-out — so the programme stays with one team and the detail stays consistent.

01

Metal Stud Partitioning

Office walls, meeting rooms and corridors framed in steel studwork to your layout — square, plumb and ready to board.

02

Plasterboarding

Single and double-layer boarding, acoustic and fire-rated systems installed to spec for clean, solid walls and ceilings.

03

Suspended Ceilings

Grid and tile ceilings set out to a true line, with cut-outs for lighting, services and access integrated neatly.

04

MF Ceilings

Seamless plasterboard MF ceilings for a flush, modern finish where a tiled grid won't do.

05

Tape & Jointing

Flat, ready-to-decorate surfaces with properly taped and jointed boards — no telegraphing, no shortcuts.

06

Doors & Vision Panels

Internal door sets, frames and glazed vision panels hung and finished to operate cleanly day one.

07

Demolition & Strip-Out

08

09

Decorating

How a fit-out comes together

From structure to finish

Every space follows the same disciplined path. Get the setting-out right and everything after it falls into line.

STEP 01

Set out & frame

Layout marked from the drawings and partitions framed in steel — true, square and on the line.

STEP 02

Services & insulate

First-fix coordinated and acoustic or thermal insulation packed in before anything is closed up.

STEP 03

Board & tape

Boarded, taped and jointed to a flat, even surface that's genuinely ready to decorate.

STEP 04

Finish & hand over

Decorated, snagged and cleaned down — a finished space handed back ready to use.

Good to know

Common questions

What types of building do you work on?

Commercial spaces — offices, industrial and warehouse units, retail and trade premises. From a single partitioned meeting room to a full unit refurbishment.

Do you cover the whole fit-out or just one trade?

Both. You can bring us in for partitioning or ceilings alone, or hand over the whole space — strip-out, partitions, ceilings, kitchens, flooring and decoration — to one team.

Can you work in an occupied or live building?

Yes. A lot of fit-out work happens around staff and trading. We plan the sequence, dust-protect and keep the site clean so you can keep operating.

How quickly can you quote?

Send the drawings or arrange a site visit and we'll get a clear, itemised quote back to you. Call 07896 832992 or email to get started.

Where are you based?

Bridgnorth in Shropshire, covering the surrounding towns and the wider West Midlands including Telford, Wolverhampton, Shrewsbury and Kidderminster.
